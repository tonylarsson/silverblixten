## Overview

This app is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data framework, or synced to iCloud. This is configurable in the settings. All data can be deleted from the app. 

## No Data Collection

This app does not collect or transmit any data to the developer, including but not limited to:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Device identifiers or advertising data
- Crash reports

Location coordinates you add to places are stored only on your device or in your private iCloud account, and are never sent to or accessible by the developer.

## No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks. It is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

## Network Communication

The app communicates exclusively with Apple's servers for the following features:

- Map and search: Apple Maps and MapKit are used for place search and displaying maps
- Route calculation: Apple's directions service is used to calculate driving routes and distances
- iCloud Sync (optional): If enabled in Settings, your trip data is synced through your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework

No data from these interactions is received or stored by the developer. All network communication is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  
## No Tracking

The app does not track you across apps or websites. It does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

## Data Deletion

All data can be deleted from the app.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Because the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
