## Overview

Turn Order Tracker is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

## Data Storage

All data — including player groups, player names, and randomization history — is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data framework. Your data is never uploaded to any server.

## No Data Collection

Turn Order Tracker does not collect any data, including but not limited to:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Device identifiers or advertising data
- Crash reports

## No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services. It is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

## No Network Communication

Turn Order Tracker does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline and makes no network requests of any kind.

## No Tracking

The app does not track you across apps or websites. It does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing groups within the app or by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

Turn Order Tracker does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Because the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
