## Contact

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter a bug or unexpected behavior, please include the following in your email:

- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce it
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 16, iPad Air)
- Your iOS version (found in Settings > General > About)

## Feature Requests

We welcome improvement ideas. Send your suggestions to the email above with "Feature Request" in the subject line.
